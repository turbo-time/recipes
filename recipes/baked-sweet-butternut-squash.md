## Ingredients
* 2 medium butternut squash

## Glaze Ingredients
* 6 tbsp butter
* 1/4 cup brown sugar
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp pepper
* Cinnamon to taste

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 400Â°.
2. Peel and cut squash into 1 1/2 inch cubes.
3. Toss cubes in glaze.
4. Cook for 50 minutes.
