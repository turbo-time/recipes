## Main Ingredients
* 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 2 eggs, beaten
* 1 cup coconut
* 1/2 cup milk
* 3/4 stick margarine (melted)
* 1 tsp vanilla

## Top Ingredients
* 1 cup light brown sugar
* 1/2 cup flour
* 3/4 stick margarine (softened)
* 1 cup chopped pecans

## Directions
Mix first seven ingredients. Pour into a greased casserole dish. Combine remaining ingredients and sprinkle on top. Bake at 350Â°F for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
