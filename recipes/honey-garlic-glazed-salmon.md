## Ingredients
* 2 salmon fillets (8oz each)

### Rub
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp pepper
* 1/2 tsp paprika (smoked)
* 1/4 tsp blackened seasoning
* Dash of Togarashi
* Garlic powder

### Sauce
* 1 tbsp Sriracha
* 1 tbsp butter
* 1 tsp olive oil
* 1/4 cup honey
* 1.5 tbsp water
* 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
* 1 tbsp ketchup

## Directions
1. Pat salmon dry and season with rub.
2. Add butter and oil to pan on MED-HI heat. Add sauce to pan. Heat through.
3. Add salmon to pan & baste for 3 minutes. = skin side down =
4. Broil for 5 min or internal temp reaches 135Â°F.
5. Garnish & Enjoy!
