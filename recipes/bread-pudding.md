## Ingredients
* 3 cups of milk
* 1/2 cup of sugar
* 3 tbsp of butter
* 1 can of biscuits
* 3 eggs
* 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp of salt
* 1 tsp of vanilla

## Topping
* 1 cup of brown sugar
* 4 tbsp butter
* 2 tbsp of milk

## Directions
1. Heat sugar, milk, butter, eggs, cinnamon, salt, and vanilla.
2. Break biscuits into bite-size pieces.
3. Pour milk mixture over biscuits.
4. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 to 60 min.
5. Heat topping ingredients and pour over pudding.
6. Broil 1 minute & serve!
