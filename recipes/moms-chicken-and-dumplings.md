## For Biscuits
* 2 cups flour
* 1/3 cup sugar
* 2 heaping tbls of mayo
* Milk (as needed)

## Directions
1. Boil chicken 10-15 min.
2. Remove chicken & add 3 chicken bouillon cubes, garlic, salt & pepper to water.
3. Let water continue to boil & drop biscuits in.
4. Add chicken when finished.
