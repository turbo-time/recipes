## Ingredients
* 4 tbsp flour
* 4 tbsp sugar
* 2 tbsp cocoa powder
* 2 tbsp vegetable oil
* 2 tbsp water
* Dash of salt

## Directions
Mix ingredients in a microwave-safe mug and microwave until done.
