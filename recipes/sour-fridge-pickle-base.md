## Ingredients
* 1 1/2 cup water
* 1 1/2 tbsp salt
* 3 tbsp vinegar
* 1/2 tbsp dill
* 1 tsp red pepper flake
* 1 tsp peppercorns

## Other tasty options
* Substitute some apple cider, white wine, rice vinegar
* 2 cloves garlic crushed and sliced
* Chopped shiso leaves
