## Ingredients
* 2 cans chickpeas
* 1 onion, diced
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 tbsp ginger, minced
* 2 tomatoes, diced
* 2 tsp cumin
* 2 tsp coriander
* 1 tsp turmeric
* 1 tsp garam masala
* 1/2 tsp cayenne
* Salt to taste
* Oil for cooking
* Fresh cilantro for garnish

## Directions
1. Heat oil in pan, saut√© onions until translucent.
2. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute.
3. Add spices, cook until fragrant.
4. Add tomatoes, cook until soft.
5. Add chickpeas and some water if needed.
6. Simmer 15-20 minutes.
7. Garnish with cilantro.
