## Ingredients
* Cabbage (chopped)
* 1 tbsp mayo
* 2 cups vinegar
* 1/6 cup sugar
* Dill
* Salt & pepper
* Onion/Garlic Powder

## Directions
Mix in a bowl and let sit.
