## Ingredients
* 1/4 cup flour
* 6 tbsp sugar
* 2 1/2 cup milk
* Dash of salt
* 4 tbsp cocoa powder
* 2 tbsp butter
* 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Directions
Mix butter w/ dry ingredients. Mix in milk slowly. Mix until it thickens over med/high heat. Add vanilla and stir.
