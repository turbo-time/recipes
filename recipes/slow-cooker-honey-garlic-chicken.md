## Servings
4 servings

## Ingredients
* 4 chicken breasts
* 16 oz of red baby potatoes, halved
* 16 oz baby carrots
* Parsley, garlic, basil
* 1/2 cup soy sauce
* 1/2 cup honey
* Onion (optional)
* 1/4 cup ketchup
* 1/4 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

## Directions
1. Cook on high in crock pot for 2-4 hours, 7-8 for low.
